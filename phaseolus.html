Phaseolus acutifolius

Phaseolus acutifolius, the Tepary bean, is a plant native to the southwestern United States and Mexico and has been cultivated by indigenous populations since pre-Columbian times. It is more drought tolerant than the common bean and is grown in desert and semi-desert conditions from Arizona through Mexico to Costa Rica.
